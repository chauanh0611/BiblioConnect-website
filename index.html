Introduction



Public libraries have widely been regarded as a wholesome source of entertainment and an integral factor in improving community literacy. In a community, they make their presence in peopleâ€™s everyday life with the curated collection of books, unlimited Internet access, research support, and book events. Therefore, understanding the use of their services can have a significant impact on the growth of our communities here in Central New York. In particular, this research project highlights the correlation between economy, education and library usage. With the assistance of exploratory data analysis and visualization, this project shed light on the impact do public libraries have on communities in Central New York.



Research Topic



Man-made disasters like COVID-19 have affected millions of people around the world. Under the guidance of Professor Junyi Jessy Li (the University of Texas at Austin) and Professor Cornelia Caragea (University of Illinois at Chicago), I will investigate how people react to these disasters through their Tweets. At the end of the summer, I will hopefully be able to improve an existing BERT model, as well as to analyze the shift in the emotion distribution over the time.
